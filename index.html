Understanding the Process: What You Can Expect

If you’ve never experienced laser hair removal, let us walk you through what happens at our Lubbock clinic. We’ve helped thousands of clients across Texas, and our process is designed with both results and comfort in mind.

Step 1: The Initial Consultation

We start with a one-on-one consultation to determine if laser hair removal is right for you. We’ll ask about your skin tone, hair type, past treatments, and current skincare routine. From there, we create a plan tailored just for you.

Step 2: Test Patch (If Needed)

Depending on your skin and hair type, we may perform a test patch to check for any sensitivities. This helps ensure that the laser reacts well with your skin before we proceed with full sessions.

Step 3: The Treatment

The procedure is usually quick—ranging from just 15 minutes for small areas to about an hour for larger zones. You’ll feel a slight snap (similar to a rubber band), but our team always checks in to make sure you’re comfortable throughout the process.

Step 4: Aftercare

Post-treatment care is crucial. We’ll give you specific instructions, which often include avoiding sun exposure and skipping certain skincare products for a few days. You’ll also receive tips to soothe any minor redness.

Step 5: Follow-Up Sessions

Most people need between 6–8 sessions for best results, spaced a few weeks apart. Hair grows in cycles, and laser treatments target follicles at a specific stage, so timing is important.

Common Questions We Get in Our Clinic

Q: Does laser hair removal hurt?
A: Everyone’s pain tolerance is different, but most describe the sensation as a quick snap. Many find it less painful than waxing.

Q: Is it safe for dark skin tones?
A: Yes, we use technology that allows us to adjust settings for a variety of skin tones. During your consultation, we’ll evaluate the safest and most effective approach for your skin.

Q: How long until I see results?
A: Most people start seeing less hair after 2–3 sessions, with full results after about 6–8 sessions.

Q: Can I shave between treatments?
A: Yes! In fact, we encourage shaving before each session. Just avoid waxing or plucking since those methods remove the hair from the root, which interferes with the laser.
